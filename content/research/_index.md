## Research Agenda

My research examines how labor market policies affect workers and firms. I use applied microeconometric methods and natural experiments to identify causal effects.

### Current Projects

**Labor Market Regulation**
- How do minimum wage policies affect employment and hours?
- What are the distributional effects of labor market institutions?

**Education and Human Capital**
- Returns to education in changing labor markets
- The role of vocational training in career outcomes

**Policy Evaluation**
- Evaluating the impact of active labor market programs
- Designing effective social insurance systems
